Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Short version: PexXel Clarity Engine applies visual filters locally on your device. We collect no browsing data. To verify your license we use your Google account email. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe — we never see your card details.

1. Who we are

PexXel Clarity Engine ("the extension", "we", "us") is a Chrome browser extension developed by Raphael Ordson, operating as PexXel Team.

Contact: pexxelteam@gmail.com

2. Data we do NOT collect

The extension does not collect or transmit:

  • Your browsing history or visited URLs
  • Page content, text, images, or any data from websites you visit
  • Passwords, form data, or login credentials
  • Your IP address or precise location
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond your Google account email (used solely for license verification — see Section 5)

3. Data stored locally on your device

The extension stores the following in chrome.storage.local on your device only:

  • Your selected enhancement profile (e.g. Balanced, Cinema)
  • Whether the extension is enabled or disabled
  • An anonymous randomly generated client ID used solely for Google Analytics 4 event counting
  • A cached license status (licensed / trial days remaining) refreshed at least every hour

None of this data is shared with any third party except as described in Sections 4 and 5.

4. Anonymous usage analytics (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 Measurement Protocol to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to help improve the extension. What is sent:

  • A random non-identifiable client ID (generated locally, no link to your identity)
  • Session ID (resets when you close Chrome)
  • Anonymous event names only: extension installed, profile selected, toggle used, popup opened
  • No URLs, no page content, no personal data

Data is sent via HTTPS to www.google-analytics.com. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Google account email — license verification

PexXel Clarity Engine includes a 7-day free trial. After the trial period, a one-time lifetime license fee of £5 GBP is required to continue using the extension.

To verify your license, the extension uses the Chrome Identity API (chrome.identity) to obtain your Google account email address. This email is used exclusively to:

  • Check your trial status against our license server (Cloudflare Worker)
  • Link your Stripe payment to your license so you can restore access on any device by signing in with the same Google account
We do not store your email permanently. Your email is used as a lookup key in our license database (Cloudflare KV). We store only: your email (as a key), the date your trial started, and whether a valid payment has been made. No other personal data is stored. Your email is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for marketing.

The Chrome Identity API only requests your email address. We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail messages, contacts, or any other Google services.

6. Payments — Stripe

All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS certified payment processor. When you purchase a license:

  • You are redirected to a Stripe-hosted checkout page
  • Stripe collects your payment details (card number, billing address)
  • We never see, store, or have access to your card details
  • Stripe sends us a confirmation webhook containing only: your email address and a transaction ID
  • We use this to activate your license in our database

Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

Stripe's security standards: stripe.com/docs/security

7. Trial period and license terms

  • Free trial: 7 days from first use, full functionality
  • After trial: Extension functionality is disabled until a license is purchased
  • License: One-time payment of £5 GBP, lifetime access
  • License binding: Tied to your Google account email — sign in with the same account on any device to restore access
  • Trial counting: Trial days are tracked server-side (Cloudflare) and cannot be reset by reinstalling the extension
  • Refunds: Contact us at pexxelteam@gmail.com within 14 days of purchase

8. How the extension works technically

PexXel Clarity Engine applies GPU-accelerated CSS filters and SVG convolution matrices (Unsharp Mask algorithm) directly in your browser. All visual processing occurs entirely on your device. No page content is ever read, copied, or transmitted to any server.

9. Chrome permissions explained

  • activeTab — Apply visual filters to the currently active tab when you interact with the extension
  • storage — Save your profile preferences and license cache locally on your device
  • scripting — Inject the GPU filter engine into web pages
  • identity — Obtain your Google account email for license verification only (see Section 5)

We request only the minimum permissions necessary. We do not request broad host permissions or access to your browsing history.

10. Cloudflare Workers — license server

Our license verification server runs on Cloudflare Workers, a serverless compute platform. When the extension checks your license status or processes a payment confirmation, a request is sent to our Cloudflare Worker at:

https://oce-license-api.mr-orda-uk.workers.dev

This server receives your Google email address and returns your license status. Cloudflare may process this request in accordance with their privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy
