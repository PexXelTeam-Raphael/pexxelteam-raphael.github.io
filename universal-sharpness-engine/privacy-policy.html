Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Short version: Universal Sharpness Engine does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. All visual processing happens locally on your device using your GPU. We never see your browsing activity.

1. Who we are

Universal Sharpness Engine is a Chrome browser extension developed by Raphael Ordson, operating as PexXel Team. Contact: pexxelteam@gmail.com

2. Data we do NOT collect

The extension does not collect or transmit:

  • Your browsing history or visited URLs
  • Page content, text, or images from any website
  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, location, IP address)
  • Login credentials or form data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Web browsing activity of any kind

3. What is stored locally

The extension stores only preferences locally using chrome.storage.local: your selected profile, on/off state, and an anonymous random client ID for GA4 event counting only — never linked to your identity.

4. Anonymous usage analytics (Google Analytics 4)

The extension uses GA4 Measurement Protocol for anonymous aggregated statistics. Sent: a random non-identifiable client ID, session ID (resets on browser close), anonymous event names only (installed, profile selected, toggle used, popup opened). No URLs, no page content, no personal data. Sent via HTTPS to www.google-analytics.com. Google privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. How the extension works

USE applies GPU-accelerated CSS filters and SVG convolution matrices (Unsharp Mask) directly in your browser. All processing happens entirely on your device. No page content is ever read, copied, or transmitted to any server.

6. Permissions explained

  • activeTab — Apply visual filters to the active tab only when you interact with the extension
  • storage — Save your profile preferences locally
  • scripting — Inject the GPU filter engine into web pages

Minimum permissions only. No host_permissions, no browsing history access.
